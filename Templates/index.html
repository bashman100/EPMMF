The Estes Park Mountain Music Festival on Saturday, May 13 at the Estes Park Events Complex will be a first annual musical extravaganza featuring national and local bands and fun for everyone.  The festival is an all day event from 12 – 9pm, with plenty of time to enjoy lots of great music, food and beverages, vendors and family friendly activities.

Lodging packages are coming soon, so make it a weekend getaway and soak up all the fun!
